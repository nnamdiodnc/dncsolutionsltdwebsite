Our Method

The standards behind every clean

Most cleans look fine on the day. The differences show up over weeks: dust that's been spread instead of removed, surfaces that streak in side-light, allergens that recirculate every time the vacuum runs. These are the practices we use to make sure they don't.

✦

Colour-coded microfibre system

Red cloths for sanitary areas, blue for kitchens, green for living spaces, yellow for low-risk dusting. Nothing crosses zones. Cross-contamination is the single biggest source of bad cleans in our industry, and we engineer it out.

✦

HEPA-filter vacuums on every job

High-street vacuums recirculate dust below 2.5 microns: the size that matters most for allergens and asthma. HEPA-filter vacuums capture and contain it. Important if anyone in the home has allergies, asthma, or young children.

✦

Top-to-bottom, back-to-front

Gravity wins. We clean ceilings, high surfaces, and shelves first, then mid-height, then floors. We work outwards from the room furthest from the door. By the time we leave, nothing we've cleaned has been re-soiled.

✦

Two-cloth finish on polished surfaces

First cloth lifts dirt and product residue. Second cloth dries and polishes. No streaks on glass, stainless steel, or polished wood. Skipping this step is why most cleaned surfaces still streak when daylight catches them at an angle.

✦

Your choice on chemistry

We carry both. Eco-grade products (Method, Ecover and equivalents) for clients who prefer low-impact formulations. Professional trade chemistry (HG, Cif Pro) where heavy carbon, limescale, or set-in stains need real solvents. You tell us your preference; we don't decide for you.

✦

Documented finish, same day

Final walkthrough against a written checklist before we leave. Photographic record of completed areas sent to you the same day. If anything is short of standard, we know about it before you do, and we come back to put it right free within 72 hours.
